ABOUT US

At ML Kargo Padala, we see ourselves as the 'Tulay ng Bayan', a company that bridges the gap between Filipinos and their loved ones, and between local businesses and their customers. With over 1,800 branches all over the country, we found an opportunity to narrow down this gap even more with ML Kargo Padala. Now people can send not only money but also packages faster and more reliably than ever before.

HOW IT WORKS

To receive a package sent via ML kargo Padala, please present two (2) valid government-issued IDs (e.g. driver’s license, SSS ID, passport) to the delivery person or to the M. Lhuillier branch personnel (for branch pick ups).
